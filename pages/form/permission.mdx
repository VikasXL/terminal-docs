# formPermission

## ensure

```js filename="Terminal" copy
ensure formPermission default:"invisible | read | writeOnce | write"
ensure formPermission allowToPrintForm:"true | false | yes | no"
ensure formPermission commentRoles:"..."
ensure formPermission commentReadOnlyRoles:"..."
ensure formPermission role:"..." permission:"invisible | read | writeOnce | write"
```

**`commentRoles`**
 : null | @empty | $Public, $Manager, $GrandManager, $AllManagers, $Assistants, $AllAssistants, $Self, Role1, Role2, ...

**`commentReadOnlyRoles`**
 : null | @empty | $Public, $Manager, $GrandManager, $AllManagers, $Assistants, $AllAssistants, $Self, Role1, Role2, ...

**`role`**
 : Role | $Public | $Manager | $GrandManager | $AllManagers | $Assistants | $AllAssistants | $Self


## remove

```js filename="Terminal" copy
remove formPermission role:"..."
```

**`role`**
 : Role | $Public | $Manager | $GrandManager | $AllManagers | $Assistants | $AllAssistants | $Self


## print

```js filename="Terminal" copy
print formPermission
```
