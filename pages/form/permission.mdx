# formPermission

## ensure

```js filename="Terminal" copy
 ensure formPermission role:"..." permission:"invisible | read | writeOnce | write" default:"invisible | read | writeOnce | write" allowToPrintForm:"true | false | yes | no" commentRoles:"..."
```

**`role`**
Role | $Public | $Manager | $GrandManager | $AllManagers | $Assistants | $AllAssistants | $Self ...

**`commentRoles`**
null | @empty | Role1, Role2, $Public, $Manager, $GrandManager, $AllManagers, $Assistants, $AllAssistants, $Self, ...


## remove
